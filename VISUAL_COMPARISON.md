## The Critical Difference

### Original: "What am I supposed to do?"
Players are dropped into a complex level with no explanation. The shadow projection mechanic is not obvious. Even if they complete it, they might not understand WHY it works.

### Enhanced: "Aha! I get it!"
Players are guided through three progressive lessons that build understanding:
1. First, learn movement and "stay in light"
2. Then, meet your shadow and see projection
3. Finally, use your shadow to solve a puzzle

By Tutorial 3, players have the **realization moment** that validates the concept.
